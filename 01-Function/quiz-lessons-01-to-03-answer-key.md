18.

$$
q(-x)=(-x)^4-5(-x)^2=x^4-5x^2=q(x)
$$

So $q(x)$ is even.

19.

$$
s(-x)=\frac{(-x)^3-4(-x)}{(-x)^2+2}=\frac{-x^3+4x}{x^2+2}
$$

$$
\frac{-x^3+4x}{x^2+2}=-\frac{x^3-4x}{x^2+2}=-s(x)
$$

So $s(x)$ is odd.

20.

$$
t(-x)=(-x)^3+(-x)^2=-x^3+x^2
$$

This is not equal to $t(x)=x^3+x^2$ and not equal to $-t(x)=-x^3-x^2$.

So $t(x)$ is neither.
