# Quiz: Lessons 1-3 Review

## Instructions

This quiz is designed for about 30 minutes.

- Part A: Choose the best answer.
- Part B: Show your work clearly.
- Use function notation whenever possible.

## Part A: Multiple Choice

1. If

$$
f(x)=3x^2-2
$$

then $f(-2)=$

A. $8$

B. $10$

C. $12$

D. $14$

2. The domain of

$$
g(x)=\frac{5}{x+3}
$$

is

A. all real numbers except $3$

B. all real numbers except $-3$

C. all real numbers greater than $-3$

D. all real numbers less than $-3$

3. The range of

$$
h(x)=\sqrt{x-4}
$$

is

A. $(-\infty,\infty)$

B. $[4,\infty)$

C. $[0,\infty)$

D. $(-\infty,0]$

4. Let

$$
p(x)=
\begin{cases}
x+4, & x<1 \\
2x-1, & x\ge 1
\end{cases}
$$

Then $p(1)=$

A. $0$

B. $1$

C. $2$

D. $5$

5. Let

$$
f(x)=2x+1,\qquad g(x)=x^2
$$

Then $(f\circ g)(-2)=$

A. $5$

B. $7$

C. $9$

D. $11$

6. Let

$$
f(x)=2x+1,\qquad g(x)=x^2
$$

Then $(g\circ f)(1)=$

A. $3$

B. $4$

C. $7$

D. $9$

7. If

$$
f(x)=-4x+8
$$

then

A. $f^{-1}(x)=2-\frac{x}{4}$

B. $f^{-1}(x)=\frac{x+8}{4}$

C. $f^{-1}(x)=-4x+8$

D. $f^{-1}(x)=\frac{8-x}{2}$

8. The function

$$
q(x)=(x+3)^2
$$

is decreasing on

A. $(-\infty,-3]$

B. $[-3,\infty)$

C. $(-\infty,\infty)$

D. $[0,\infty)$

9. The function

$$
r(x)=x^5-2x
$$

is

A. even

B. odd

C. neither

D. constant

10. On the interval $[0,\pi]$, the function

$$
s(x)=\cos x
$$

is

A. increasing

B. decreasing

C. constant

D. neither increasing nor decreasing

## Part B: Free Response

11. Let

$$
f(x)=
\begin{cases}
2x-3, & x<0 \\
x^2+1, & x\ge 0
\end{cases}
$$

Find:

- $f(-2)$
- $f(0)$
- $f(3)$

12. Let

$$
f(x)=x-4,\qquad g(x)=3x+2
$$

Find:

- $(f\circ g)(x)$
- $(g\circ f)(x)$

13. Let

$$
k(x)=4x-9
$$

Find $k^{-1}(x)$. Show your algebra steps.

14. Let

$$
h(x)=(x-2)^2,\qquad x\ge 2
$$

Find $h^{-1}(x)$.

15. Let

$$
m(x)=(x+1)^2,\qquad x\le -1
$$

Find $m^{-1}(x)$.

16. Consider

$$
p(x)=-(x-1)^2+4
$$

State:

- where $p(x)$ is increasing
- where $p(x)$ is decreasing

17. Consider

$$
r(x)=x^2-6x+5
$$

State:

- where $r(x)$ is increasing
- where $r(x)$ is decreasing

18. Determine whether

$$
q(x)=x^4-5x^2
$$

is even, odd, or neither. Show the test using $q(-x)$.

19. Determine whether

$$
s(x)=\frac{x^3-4x}{x^2+2}
$$

is even, odd, or neither. Show the test using $s(-x)$.

20. Determine whether

$$
t(x)=x^3+x^2
$$

is even, odd, or neither. Show the test using $t(-x)$.
